Shenandoah Sovereign - Brand Identity

Creating a brand system for Virginia hospitality.

Shenandoah Sovereign is a catering and wedding brand rooted in Virginia's Blue Ridge region. The identity needed a lockup, monogram, and botanical support system that felt warm, refined, and grounded in the landscape.

Brand.
Identity system includes a primary mark, wordmark, monogram variations, and botanical illustrations ready for editorial venue materials.

Botanica Lipport

Native Blue Ridge flora and fauna illustrated to complement the visual identity including cardinals, branch dogwood, and Trillium sessile, rendered with clean line work and subtle color.

Botanical Support

Native Blue Ridge flora and fauna designed as supportive brand elements, including cardinals, branch dogwood, and Trillium sessile, rendered to serve print details and softer accent uses.

Color Exploration and lockup

We explored palettes inspired by seasonal harvest and the natural landscape. The final selection lands in a muted terracotta range balanced with botanical green and harvest gold for warmth and softness.

Refining the monogram and lockup

Monogram & Lockup
Custom monogram assets intertwine S's for Shenandoah Sovereign. The full lockup elegantly combines the wordmark with botanical illustration for versatility in badges and branded style.

Exploring multiple directions before refining

Early logo options were sketched, concepted, and digitally iterated to expose different monogram, lockup, and illustration styles before selecting a refined final identity system.
