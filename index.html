About Me

Designer & Builder

I'm a designer with roots in print and a deep pull toward the web - drawn to the kind of work where layout, texture, and type come together to make something feel considered. I've spent 10+ years working across brand identity, UX/UI, eCommerce, and production design, and I still find the most satisfaction in projects that evolve until they feel just right.

I'm based on the East Coast and work remotely with teams and clients who care about the details.
